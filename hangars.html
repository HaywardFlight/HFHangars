Hangar Information

The Hangar

Our hangar offers secure and convenient parking for various aircraft, including single and twin engine models, as well as jets. Located in Hayward, California, our facility provides a safe and reliable space for aircraft owners to store their planes. We prioritize the security and safety of your aircraft and have implemented comprehensive measures to the hangar and its surroundings.

Our hangar is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, ensuring that we are always available to meet your needs. However, to provide you with the best service possible, we request a 24-hour notice via phone or email before your flight. This allows us ample time to prepare and ensure everything is ready for you.

What we offer​

  • Lounge space
  • Complementary wifi
  • Fire sprinklers, fire alarms and a fire extinguisher in each unit.
  • Safe and secure storage for every aircraft
  • Three-phase power available for everyone. A three-phase power outlet may be installed in each unit.
  • Restrooms available for tenants​
  • 24-hour access via secure gate.
  • Ample private parking in the driveway. Parking spaces inside the fence available for rent

INCLUDED SERVICES

Towing

We want you to know that when choose our hangars, you're getting top-notch towing services for your aircraft. Our skilled team will expertly pull your aircraft out for an exciting day of flying and ensure it’s returned safely to the hangar afterward. We will handle every detail, so you can focus on enjoying your time in the air.

Getting Ready

We know that preparing for a flight can be a hassle, especially when it comes to managing your gear. That's why we provide lockers specifically designed for your pre-flight items, such as oil or air pumps. Store your equipment securely and have peace mind as you get ready to take off.

Repair Shop

In the event that your plane requires a trip to the repair shop, rest assured that we are here to help. We will efficiently transport your plane to and from the repair shop across the airport, ensuring a smooth process for you. Your aircraft's maintenance is our priority, and we aim to minimize any inconvenience during this time. Trust us to handle the logistics while you focus on your flying experience.
